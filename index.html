Welcome to the SPRING Meeting

The scope of the workshop includes, but is not limited to:

  • Design of symmetric-key primitives over prime fields and integer rings for applications such as Multi-Party Computation (MPC), Homomorphic Encryption (HE), Zero-Knowledge (ZK), and Format-Preserving Encryption (FPE);
  • New or improved cryptanalysis techniques;
  • Implementation of symmetric-key primitives over prime fields and integer rings.
  • The program contains invited talks and discussion sessions.
organised by
  • Lorenzo Grassi (Technology University of Einhoven, the Netherlands) and
  • Marco Pedicini (Roma Tre University).
